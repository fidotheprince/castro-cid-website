Calorie Counting App
This application was built based on a tutorial during my first six months of learning code. The importance of this application can not be overlooked in my growth as a developer.

Within the construction of the application, I learned the importance of functional programming concepts like currying, closures, and partial functions. Also, this was one of the first times that I utilized a switch statement in production.

Admittedly, however, I learned more about what not to do when it comes to being a developer.

Creating an application is more than just learning from a tutorial and copying what the instructor did. Being a developer is about knowing what you are building and adapting a complimentary technology to construct said project.

At the time, my understanding of what a developer did, why certain technologies work better for certain projects, what a library is, what a framework is, these things took time for me to grasp. Once I realized that an excellent developer is a problem solver, and his greatest tool is not the language or technology they use, but their ability to make something of value with that technology, this is when I was ready to take on my first originally built project.
